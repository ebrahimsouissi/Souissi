What I do · How I price it

Services & rates.

Prices below are starting points, not ceilings. Each project is scoped after a short conversation. Tunisia-based clients pay in TND; international clients in USD or EUR.

01

Logo Design

A clean, considered logo built around your business. Three concepts, one final mark, and all the export files you'll ever need.

  • 3 concept directions
  • 2 rounds of revision
  • Final logo in all formats (AI, PDF, PNG, SVG)
  • Black, white, and color variants
  • 5–10 day turnaround
Starting at 150 TND ~$50 USD · per logo
02

Brand Basics

Logo plus the building blocks of a brand: color palette, typography, and a one-page guideline doc to keep things consistent.

  • Logo + variants
  • Color system & typography
  • Mini brand guidelines (1–2 pages)
  • Social media profile assets
  • 2 week turnaround
Starting at 400 TND ~$130 USD · per project
03

Social Media Pack

A pack of post designs ready to publish — Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook. Editable templates included so you can keep building.

  • 10 custom post designs
  • 1 carousel (up to 6 slides)
  • Editable Canva or Figma templates
  • Story templates included
Starting at 200 TND ~$65 USD · per pack
04

Print & Menu Design

Restaurant menus, event flyers, posters, and business cards. Print-ready files delivered with bleed and crop marks.

  • Menu design (1–4 pages)
  • Posters & flyers
  • Business card design
  • Print-ready PDF + source files
Starting at 150 TND ~$50 USD · per piece
05

Illustration

Custom illustration — for brands, social posts, posters, or just because. Vector or raster, depending on the brief.

  • Custom illustration
  • Vector or raster output
  • Up to 2 rounds of revision
  • Source files included
Starting at 100 TND ~$30 USD · per illustration
06

Data Visualization

Where my two worlds meet. Infographics, dashboard mockups, presentation visuals, and report design — clear, accurate, beautiful.

  • Infographics & data illustrations
  • Presentation design (PowerPoint / Keynote)
  • Dashboard visual design
  • Annual reports & one-pagers
Starting at 250 TND ~$80 USD · per project
How we work · Step by step

The process.

01

Brief

A quick call or message to understand your business, your audience, and what you actually need. No charge for this part.

02

Quote

Within 24–48 hours: a clear quote with scope, timeline, and a fixed price. No surprises, no upselling.

03

Design

Concepts shared along the way, not at the end. You see the work as it develops, with room for honest feedback.

04

Delivery

All final files delivered (AI, PNG, PDF, SVG, source files). Plus a 14-day window for small adjustments.
