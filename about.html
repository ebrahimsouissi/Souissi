— Bonjour, je suis Ibrahim.

I'm a graphic designer based in Tunis, working with founders, editors, and creative teams across North Africa, Europe, and the Gulf. I started designing professionally in 2020 — first inside a small branding studio, then on my own.

My work sits at the intersection of typography, identity, and rhythm. I'm most useful when a brand needs a complete visual system: not just a logo, but a way of seeing, naming, and arranging things that holds up across print, motion, and the web.

I care about craft and clarity in equal measure. I think every detail is a decision and every decision should mean something. I'd rather design less, slower, and more intentionally than ship a generic system that disappears into the noise.

Outside of client work, I'm interested in editorial design, Arabic and Latin type pairing, and the quiet politics of visual language. I read more than I should and sleep less than I should — both habits I'm trying to fix.
