— Bonjour, je suis Ebrahim.

I'm a graphic designer based in Tunisia, currently splitting my time between data analysis and visual design. I hold a Licence in Informatique de Gestion from ISIMa Mahdia (2019–2023), and I've been building design work since 2022 — first inside the Microsoft ISIMa Club, then as a freelancer for small businesses and personal briefs.

My day job is data — I work as a Data Analyst and PMO at AS2E Automation, where I spend my time turning numbers into decisions. My evenings and weekends are for design.

That double life shapes how I work. I think about audiences, goals, and outcomes the way a data person does — not just how a layout looks, but whether it does its job. I also care about craft: typography, spacing, and the quiet decisions that separate a logo that disappears from one that holds up.

I'm at the start of my freelance journey, and I'm selective on purpose. I want to do good work for clients I respect, not chase volume. If your project sounds interesting and the fit is right, I'd love to hear about it.
